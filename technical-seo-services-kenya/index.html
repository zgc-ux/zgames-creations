What Is Technical SEO?

Think of Technical SEO as the game engine behind your website.

It’s the invisible system that allows Google to crawl, understand, and rank your site correctly. Without it, even the best content, branding, or marketing cannot perform at full power.

Technical SEO covers site speed, mobile optimization, structured data, security, crawl control, and performance — the core mechanics that determine whether your website wins or loses in search results.
